About us

"Our purpose is to connect business and make them evolve."
Mark Sounders
CEO and Founder of LinkedHospitality

Who we are

Our CEO and founder Mark Sounders has worked in the most prestigious Hotels in the UK starting from Bartender until progress his way up to being the General Manager for one of the biggest and most important Hotel groups in Europe. Mak has also won two Catey awards as "Wine and Spirit Ambassador", and "Manager of the year". After an extended career in Bar restaurants and Hotels, he decided to fulfil his dream to create LinkedHospitality along with some of his fellow colleges.

"Our events are full of experiences and inspiration for your business."
Mayra Morgan
Events Director of LinkedHospitality.

What we do

We create events tailored to the needs of the business, considering new technologies and innovations that will create better service, more significant revenue and an excellent reputation to our members. Those events offer opportunities to our operators and suppliers to fulfil those gaps, do business, improve their services by learning from others in the industry, showcase what they do best and find the best solution for their businesses.

"We are here to help you connect and succeed."
Josh Harrison
Business Development Director of LinkedHospitality.

How we do it

We host three yearly events; each of them focusing on different themes and unique suppliers targeted to our member's needs. We plan our events thoroughly and strategically based on the gaps presented in the industry; our business development team works hard on researching and designing each experience. Each one of our events has the expertise you need to make your business thrive.
